Software estimation is now easier

Evaluate the aspects of a task to estimate it better

💪

Effort

Will the team need a high amount of time or resources to complete it?
⚠️

Risk

What external aspects endanger this task?
🧮

Complexity

How hard is this to understand, both technically and from a domain point of view? How many components are involved?
🥴

Uncertainty

What are there unknowns? How well does the team know the code base, each other’s skills, the technology, the business process?
